Maggie Recipe

plain and simple Maggie

Ingredients

  • Maggie pack
  • 1 1/2 cup water
  • Any vegies you want to add for a personal touch

Instructions

  1. Take out the maggie noodles and the masala pack provided in the maggie pack
  2. put the above in a pan with the water
  3. put it on the stove to heat and keep mixing regularly
  4. Once there is very little water left take turn off the stove and enjoy your maggie
